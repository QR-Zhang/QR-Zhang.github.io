I am currently an assoicate professor at the School of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Sun Yat-sen University, China. Before joining Sun Yat-Sen University,  I was a post-doctoral researcher from 2019-2020 at Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), Delft, the Netherlands.  I received my Ph.D. degree in Aerospace Science and Engineering at 2019 from the Institute for Aerospace Studies (UTIAS), University of Toronto, under the supervision of Prof. Hugh H.T. Liu. Before that, I obtained my Bachelor degree in Control Engineering from the Department of Control Science and Engineering, Harbin Institute of Technology (HIT) at 2013.
